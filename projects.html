Our Work


Covid Prison Data is an effort centered on developing and maintaining automated tools that collect publicly-available data from websites about COVID-19 in prisons. This includes facility-level (when available) information in most state and federal prisons in the United States, and is collected on a daily basis. This project provides high-quality, timely, and freely accessible data on how COVID-19 is affecting in some of the largest known COVID vectors, prisons
